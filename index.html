About This Site

Abuja Weather delivers daily, hype-free forecasts for the Federal Capital Territory — written in plain language, with no panic and no filler.

Weather data is sourced from Open-Meteo (ECMWF IFS model). Because Nigeria has limited ground radar, forecasts rely on satellite-derived precipitation data and numerical weather models.

No hype. Just the forecast.
